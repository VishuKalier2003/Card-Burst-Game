Burst Game
    The Rules are pretty Simple...
  • Block will be thrown onto the screen...
  • You have to break the block before crossing the screen...
  • You can break block by clicking only once on the block...
  • You will have 5 lives...
  • When broken the block will shrink and disappear...
  • Every Block breaking awards you 2 points...
